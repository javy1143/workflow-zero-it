About

Built by someone who understands real IT chaos.

Workflow Zero IT exists to help small teams simplify operations, automate repetitive work, and use AI in practical ways that make everyday work easier.

Founder story

From support tickets to smoother systems.

Workflow Zero IT was founded by Javi, a helpdesk manager with infrastructure experience and a passion for automation, AI, and cleaner systems.

After years of supporting users, troubleshooting systems, and seeing the same manual processes break down again and again, the mission became clear: small businesses need more than basic tech support. They need systems that reduce friction.

The goal is simple: make technology easier to operate, easier to trust, and more useful in the day-to-day work of running a business.

Working principles

Clean, practical, modern, trustworthy, direct.

Practical first

We focus on workflows your team will actually use, not unnecessary complexity.

Security-aware

Access, permissions, data awareness, and documentation are part of the work from the start.

Tool-conscious

The goal is not more software. The goal is to make your existing tools work together better.

What makes us different

Traditional IT support keeps the lights on. Workflow Zero IT helps the business run better by combining support experience with workflow automation and practical AI.

What we avoid

  • Overbuilt systems your team cannot maintain
  • AI experiments without clear guardrails
  • Automations that hide broken processes
  • Tool sprawl disguised as innovation
