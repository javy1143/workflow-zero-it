Good fit

Choose IT Operations when support is the immediate need.

  • Users need help but you do not need a full MSP contract
  • Patch compliance is inconsistent or invisible
  • Vulnerability findings are piling up without owners
  • Onboarding and offboarding are too manual
  • Permissions, groups, and shared mailboxes need cleanup

Scope

Clear boundaries keep the service useful.

  • Remote-first support during business hours
  • On-site work scoped separately when needed
  • Security tools and software licenses billed separately
  • Projects, automations, and AI buildouts quoted separately
  • Emergency response availability depends on the selected plan
