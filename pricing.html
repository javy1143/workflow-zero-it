How to choose

Choose the path that matches the immediate pain.

Start with a Workflow Audit when the process is unclear. Choose IT Operations Only when the priority is support, patch compliance, access cleanup, or vulnerability remediation.

Good fit signals

  • Your team repeats the same admin steps every week
  • Follow-ups or handoffs get missed
  • You are unsure how employees are using AI
  • Your current tools work, but the process around them does not
  • Your users need IT help but not a full automation project
